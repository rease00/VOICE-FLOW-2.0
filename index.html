FAQ

Common questions, straight answers.

What is Voice Flow?

Voice Flow is a web-based voice production workspace. You write scripts, assign AI voices, direct delivery with prompts, and review rendered audio — all in one tool.

What languages are supported?

The Prime engine supports 30+ languages including English, Hindi, Spanish, Japanese, Arabic, French, German, and more. New languages are added as the upstream model expands.

How does billing work?

You purchase VF Credits in packs or via a subscription plan. Credits are consumed per generation — no hidden fees, no monthly minimum. You only pay for what you use.

Can I use multiple voices in one scene?

Yes. Multi-speaker mode lets you assign different voices to different speakers in a single script and render the entire scene in one pass.

Is there a free tier?

Pricing plans start at ₹129/month with credits included. Token top-up packs are also available if you need more without committing to a larger plan.

Who builds this?

V FLOW AI is a solo-built product focused on doing a few things well rather than promising everything. Updates ship frequently based on real usage.

Ready to start?

Open the studio and render your first scene.

Voice Flow keeps everything in one workspace — no installs, no file juggling, no setup guides. Sign in, paste a script, and press render.
